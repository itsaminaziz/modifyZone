FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is Grammarly safe to use with confidential documents?
Yes, Grammarly takes security seriously. Your text is encrypted during transmission and processed securely. Grammarly does not sell your data or share it with third parties. You can also use Grammarly offline for sensitive documents.
What platforms does Grammarly support?
Grammarly works on Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, and as a browser extension for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. It also integrates with Microsoft Office, Google Docs, and many other writing applications.
What's the difference between free and premium Grammarly?
Free Grammarly provides basic grammar and spelling checks. Premium adds advanced features like clarity suggestions, vocabulary enhancement, plagiarism detection, and writing style improvements for professional communication.
Can students use Grammarly for academic writing?
Absolutely! Grammarly is widely used by students for essays, research papers, and academic writing. The plagiarism checker helps ensure originality, while writing suggestions improve clarity and academic tone.
Does Grammarly support multiple languages?
Currently, Grammarly primarily supports English writing. However, it can help non-native English speakers improve their English writing skills with clear explanations and educational feedback.
How do I install and set up Grammarly?
Installation is simple: download from our website, install the application or browser extension, create a free account, and start writing. Grammarly automatically begins checking your writing across supported applications.
